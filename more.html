Cakenhagen’s ice cream pastries

Cakenhagen offers a summer oddity – four cake classics have been reimagined as ice cream pastries. The ice cream is provided by the famous artisanal ice cream producer PBA Ice Cream, which makes ice creams of the most elevated quality.

THE PBA CAKE

Vanilla ice cream with apple compote, scone crunch and meringue

THE MINE

Seriously chocolate sorbet with a base of Gâteau Marcel and cocoa nibs

THE PEACOCK

Apple and orange sorbet with candied orange and vanilla cream

THE FLOWER

Lemon sorbet, disintegrate and rosehip meringue

DKK 59 per ice cream dessert

The ice cream desserts are only available at Cakenhagen on Vesterbrogade 1
